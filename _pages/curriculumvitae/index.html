About

I am a PhD Student at the Department of Signal Theory, Telematics and Communications of the University of Granada. My research focuses on wireless networks and mobile communications, particularly in the context of IoT and beyond 5G/6G systems.

In 2023, I was a visiting doctoral researcher at Simula Research Laboratory, University of Oslo, Norway.
