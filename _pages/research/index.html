Research

I am a member of the Wireless and Multimedia Networking Lab (TIC-235), part of the Andalusian Scientific Information System (S.I.C.A.), at the University of Granada. My research activity is focused on wireless networks and mobile communications, with particular interest in QoS/QoE, IoT, and beyond 5G/6G systems.

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Telecommunication Engineering at the University of Granada, under the supervision of Prof. Juan M. Lopez-Soler and Prof. Jorge Navarro-Ortiz. I currently hold one period of three years of recognized research activity (un trienio de investigación) by the Ministry of Education and Science.

In 2023, I was a visiting doctoral student under the supervision of Dr. Özgü Alay at Simula Research Laboratory, University of Oslo, Norway.

Research Interests
  • LPWAN & IoT (LoRaWAN)
  • 5G / 6G Mobile Networks
  • SDN / NFV & Network Slicing
  • Multipath Protocols (MPTCP & MPQUIC)
  • Heterogeneous Networks
  • AI/ML for Network Optimization
  • Low-latency & Deterministic Communications
  • Non-terrestrial Networks
