He received the B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees in Telecommunication Engineering from the University of Granada, Spain, in 2020 and 2022. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in Telecommunication Engineering with the Wireless and Multimedia Networking Lab Research Group.

In 2023, he was a visiting doctoral researcher at the Simula Research Laboratory, University of Oslo, Norway. His research focuses on wireless networks, IoT, and beyond 5G/6G systems.

Research Interests

  • LPWAN and IoT systems (LoRaWAN)
  • 5G/6G mobile networks and SDN/NFV
  • Multipath protocols and heterogeneous networks
  • AI/ML for network optimization
  • Low-latency and non-terrestrial networks
