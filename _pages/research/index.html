Research

I am a member of the Wireless and Multimedia Networking Lab (TIC-235) at the University of Granada, where I participate in research projects on IoT, LPWAN, and beyond 5G/6G networks.

In 2023, I was a visiting doctoral researcher at Simula Research Laboratory, University of Oslo, Norway.

  • Multipath Protocols

  • AI/ML for Network Optimization
